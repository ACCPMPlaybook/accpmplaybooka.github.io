PDx Process

Product Development Execution Process

The PDx Process outlines the structured approach for developing and launching new products, ensuring alignment with company goals, market needs, and quality standards.

This comprehensive framework guides cross-functional teams through each phase of product development, from ideation to post-launch evaluation.

Process Phases

1 Ideation & Concept Development

Generating and evaluating new product ideas based on market research, customer feedback, and business objectives.

2 Feasibility Analysis

Assessing technical, financial, and market feasibility to determine if the product concept should move forward.

3 Product Design & Development

Creating detailed product specifications, developing prototypes, and conducting initial testing.

4 Validation & Testing

Comprehensive testing to ensure the product meets quality standards, performance requirements, and user expectations.

5 Production Preparation

Finalizing manufacturing processes, supply chain arrangements, and production planning.

6 Launch & Commercialization

Coordinating product launch activities, marketing campaigns, and initial distribution.

7 Post-Launch Evaluation

Monitoring product performance, gathering customer feedback, and making necessary adjustments.
