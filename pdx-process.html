What is PDx Process?

The PDx Process is a structured framework that streamlines the development of accessory products from concept to launch and beyond. It ensures cross-functional alignment, quality control, and the timely delivery of products that meet both customer needs and business goals.

It advances through distinct stages: portfolio planning, product definition, concept validation, plan, development & qualification, launch preparation, and lifecycle management. Each stage uses specific checkpoints and deliverables to ensure quality and consistency across all products.
