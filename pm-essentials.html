What is Portfolio Planning?

Portfolio planning is a strategic process that involves selecting and managing a collection of projects, products, or services to achieve organizational goals. It ensures that resources are allocated efficiently across initiatives to maximize value, balance risk, and align with business objectives.

Effective portfolio planning helps prioritize investments, manage dependencies, and adapt to market changes, ensuring that the organization's product portfolio remains competitive and aligned with long-term strategy.
